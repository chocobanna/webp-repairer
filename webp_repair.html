WebP Region Repair

Cut Settings

Use columns/rows for big shuffled regions. For your shown image, start with something like 5×5, 6×6, 8×6, or 10×6. Don’t crank it to 200×300 unless you enjoy pain.

Auto Sort

Auto-sort compares color differences between neighboring edges. Lower edge difference means a better match. White/blank chunks are dumb and will confuse it. That’s not a bug, that’s your image having no useful information there.

Edit Mode

Swap: click two tiles on the output image.

Assign: click destination on the left, then source on the right.
